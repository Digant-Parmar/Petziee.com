3. All Breed Formula

Drools chicken and egg puppy dog food is a complete and balanced diet formulated for all breeds. It is highly palatable and loaded with goodness that any breed of canine will love.

Please ensure while receiving the product, it is not tampered or damaged. Store it in an air-tight container in a cool and dry place, away from sunlight. Avoid basements, condensation and temperature variations as it encourages mould growth, vermin and bugs. Washing the container and drying it properly before use is recommended to keep the food fresh for long time.

Add portions of Drools chicken and egg adult food into the current food (home food - dal, rice, chicken, vegetables etc).

It is found that breeds vary in body growth and development. It depends upon various factors which include activity, age, size, temperament of your pet and climate. Clean and fresh water should be available at all times. Regular exercise or any form of fun activities also maintain the body weight of your pet and keeps them more energetic and vibrant.
